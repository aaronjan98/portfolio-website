Game of Life

The Game of Life is a grid of cells that based on the count of each cell's neighbors: it will die, stay alive, or revive; this type of programmatic system is called cellular automata. The birth and death of the cells resemble processes of life from bacterial growth to the interactions at the bases of our life: protons, neutrons and electrons.

This is a javascript application that does not use any external assistance of a framework or a bundler; although, the p5 library is used for its drawing and animation functionality.
Tools Implemented:
Javascript | p5.js
